How AI Recommendations Work

Ever wonder how Netflix or YouTube knows what you like? They use AI recommendation systems! These systems look at things like:

  • What you've liked before (Genre Match)
  • What's popular overall (Popularity)
  • What others think (User Reviews)
  • Content rules (like age ratings)

The Key Insight: People design these AI systems. They decide what data to use and which factors are most important. By changing these 'priorities' (like you can with the toggles above), the AI gives different recommendations. There's no single 'correct' AI; its behavior reflects the goals and choices of its creators!
